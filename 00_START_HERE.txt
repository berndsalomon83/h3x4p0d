ğŸ”§ CUSTOMIZATION

To adapt for your robot:

1. Leg Geometry (~/.hexapod/config.json or web UI)
   leg_coxa_length = 30.0    # your leg dimensions
   leg_femur_length = 60.0
   leg_tibia_length = 80.0

2. Servo Channels (calibration tool or web UI)
   Maps each leg/joint to PCA9685 channel

3. Gait Parameters (config or profile)
   step_height = 25.0     # customize movement
   step_length = 40.0
   cycle_time = 1.2

4. Profiles (for different configurations)
   Use ProfileManager or /api/profiles endpoints
